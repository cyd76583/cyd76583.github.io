Kunming, a City of Eternal Spring

Location

Kunming, a city located at the southwest of China, is well-known for its moderate temperature throughout the year and its numerous tourist attractions. Kunming is an important regional city in southwestern as well as a gateway opening to Southeast Asia and South Asia. Took advantage of its proximity to the southeastern border of China, Kunming acts as a important transfer station to facilitate the trade with countries in Southeast Asia like Thailand, Burma and Laos. People may see a lot of exotic goods at exhibition in Kunming.

Climate

Kunming is located at a pleateau with an average elevation of 1891m relative to sea level. Kunming has a subtropical monsoon climate that gives it plenty of precipitations for agriculture. Under the impact of warm and humid air flow from southwestern Indian Ocean, Kunming enjoys a long period of sunshine with a short period of frost.The average annual temperature is 15 degress Celcius. Therefore, the summer in Kunming is not very hot while the winter is not very cold. This rare feature gives Kunming its good name of "Spring City".
